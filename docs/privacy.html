Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2025

Introduction

TBG Quote Builder ("we", "our", or "the app") is a private business application used exclusively by TBG Enterprises for creating and managing customer quotes. This privacy policy explains how we handle data.

Data Collection

The app collects and stores:

  • Customer contact information (name, email, phone) for quote creation
  • Project descriptions and quote details
  • QuickBooks Online authentication tokens for integration purposes

Data Usage

All data is used solely for:

  • Creating and managing quotes for TBG Enterprises customers
  • Syncing estimates with QuickBooks Online
  • Generating PDF quotes for customers

Data Storage

Data is stored securely and is not shared with any third parties except as necessary for QuickBooks Online integration.

Data Retention

Quote data is retained for business record-keeping purposes in accordance with applicable laws.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact TBG Enterprises.
